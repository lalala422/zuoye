Research

Research

My work lies at the interface of coding theory, combinatorics, algebra, and emerging storage technologies, especially DNA-based data storage.

01

Deletion-Correcting Codes

Bounds, constructions, and decoding algorithms for deletion channels, burst deletions, localized deletions, insertion/deletion errors, and related synchronization-error models.

02

DNA-Based Data Storage

Coding-theoretic and combinatorial models for reliable storage, reconstruction, and indexing of information in DNA-based and polymer-based storage systems.

03

Sequence Reconstruction

Reconstruction of strings from distorted observations, composition multisets, prefix-suffix information, and related combinatorial data.

04

Combinatorics and Algebra

Applications of combinatorial designs, finite fields, algebraic methods, and discrete structures in the construction and analysis of codes.
