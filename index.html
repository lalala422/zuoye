About

Biography

I am an Associate Professor at the Institute of Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Sciences, Xidian University. Before joining Xidian University, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, advised by Ohad Elishco. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematical Sciences from the University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Xiande Zhang and Gennian Ge.

Research Focus

Selected Themes

  • Deletion-correcting and synchronization-error-correcting codes.
  • Combinatorial and algebraic methods in coding theory.
  • Codes for DNA-based data storage and sequence reconstruction.
