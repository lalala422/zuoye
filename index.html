About

Biography

I am an Associate Professor at the Institute of Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Sciences , Xidian University . Before joining Xidian University, I was a postdoctoral fellow in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, advised by Ohad Elishco. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematical Sciences from the University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Xiande Zhang and Gennian Ge.

Research

Research Interests

Problems at the interface of coding theory, discrete mathematics, and emerging storage technologies.

01

Deletion-Correcting Codes

Bounds, constructions, and efficient decoding for deletion channels, burst deletions, localized deletions, and related synchronization errors.

02

DNA-Based Data Storage

Combinatorial models and codes for reliable storage and reconstruction over DNA-inspired channels and composite DNA sequence models.

03

Combinatorics and Algebra

Applications of combinatorial designs, algebraic methods, sequence reconstruction, and finite-field tools in coding theory.
